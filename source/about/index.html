Seth Davis
founder

Seth grew up in the sign business and loved to build things. At a young age he was doing metal work and went on to pursue a degree in Industrial Design from Philadelphia University. Seth quick a niche helping creative people build there visions. With over 20 years in the sign industry, Seth constantly monitors new fabrication trends, integrating them into his business plan when a quality result can be consistently guaranteed. He is a presenter and sponsor at industry conferences and is considered a resource to design firms, corporate clients and other sign fabricators.
